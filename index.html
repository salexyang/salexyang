Research Interests

My primary research interest is on the interface of Operations Management and Finance. Specific topics include:

·       Supply Chain Finance

·       Managing operations under financial constraints

·       FinTech, Financial Innovation, and Financial Inclusion

Recently, one stream of my research focuses on platform governance and operations.

My other research interests include supply chain management, risk management, and business analytics.
